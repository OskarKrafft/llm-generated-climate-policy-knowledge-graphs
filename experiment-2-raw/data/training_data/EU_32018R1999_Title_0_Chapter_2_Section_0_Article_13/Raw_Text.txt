article 13
assessment of the integrated national energy and climate plans
on the basis of the integrated national energy and climate plans and their updates as notified pursuant to articles 3 andÂ 14, the commission shall assess, in particular, whether:
(a)
the objectives, targets and contributions are sufficient for the collective achievement of the energy union objectives and, for the first ten-year period in particular, the targets of the union's 2030 climate and energy framework;
